Recipe Sharing Website

This website is a place where you can find, share, and rate recipes. Whether you're looking for a new recipe to try or want to share your own creations, this website is the perfect place for you.

Try it out!

Features

  • Find recipes from a variety of cuisines
  • Share your own recipes with the community
  • Rate and review recipes
  • Save recipes to your favorites

About

This website was created by a team of students at Holberton School. We were inspired to create this website because we love food and we wanted to create a place where people could share their love of food with others.
